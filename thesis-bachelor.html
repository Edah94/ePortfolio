NATURAL GEOGRAPHICAL TOURISM POTENTIALS OF THE MUCIPALITY OF VISOKO

The bachelor's thesis named "Natural Geographical Tourism Potentials of the Municipality of Visoko" focuses on potential tourism development strategies within the given municipality. Initially, the natural- and socio-geographical characteristics of the municipality were analyzed and subsequently all tourist attractions within the municipality were identified. Through the analysis of current situation, conceptual solutions are proposed to attempt the development or enhancement of existing tourism within the municipality. Given the existing tourist movements, these conceptual solutions should involve activities that could act as complementary elements to increase visitor numbers, length of stay, tourist expenditure, attractiveness, and more.
